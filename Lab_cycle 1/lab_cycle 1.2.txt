<h2><u>LISTS IN HTML</u></h2></br>
<ol type="1">
<li>Ordered list (Numbered list)
<li>Unordered list (Unnumbered list)
</ol>
<b><u>ORDERED LIST</u></b>
<ol type="i">
<li>List of items appear with a use of numbers or alphabets is called 'ordered list'.
<li>The attributes that can be specified with the ordered list tag is 'Type' and 'Start'.
<li>Start:Alters the numbering sequence.
<li>Type="1" :counting numbers(1,2..)
<li>Type="A" :uppercase letters(A,B..)
<li>Type="a" :lowercase letters(a,b..)
<li>Type="I" :uppercase roman numbers(I,II..)
<li>Type="i" :lowercase roman numbers(i,ii..)
</ol>
<b><u>UNORDERED LIST</u></b>
<ul type="circle">
<li>It appear as a list of items with "bullets" or markers in the front.
<li>Important attribute of an unordered list is 'Type'.
<li>values of type atttributes: Disc,Circle and Square.
</ul>
<b><u>ANOTHER LIST IN HTML</u></b>


<ul type="square">
<li>Definition list
<li>It is a list items without bullet or numbers.
<li>It is list of items and definitions
</ul>
